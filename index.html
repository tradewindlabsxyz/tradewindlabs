Building now

Tradewind Options

A desktop-first options trading interface and protocol layer for calls, puts, expiries, strikes, greeks, premiums, payoff views, and portfolio risk. Backend integrations and live trading flows will follow a separate production plan.

  • 24/7 options markets for crypto-native assets
  • Hyperliquid Core liquidity with HyperEVM extensibility
  • Clean terminal UX for retail, professional, and institutional users
  • Options-native language: calls, puts, IV, greeks, strikes, and expiries
  • Self-custody and verifiable settlement as core design constraints

Principles

Built for markets, not hype cycles.

TradeWind Labs builds infrastructure with a bias toward clarity, risk visibility, and long-term operational quality.

Δ

Risk first

Traders should see payoff, greeks, exposure, and premium impact before any future execution flow asks them to act.

↯

Fast, clean workflows

The product should feel direct and responsive, with dense information presented in a way that remains readable under pressure.

◌

Verifiable rails

Protocol design should prefer transparent onchain settlement, self-custody, and minimized trust assumptions.
